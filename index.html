Welcome To Royal College Horana

Where education meets excellence! Our school is located in the beautiful town of Horana, Sri Lanka and we are proud to offer a world-class education to our students. At Royal College Horana, we believe that every student has the potential to succeed and it is our mission to unlock that potential through quality education, innovative teaching methods, and a supportive learning environment.

Academics

Royal College, located in Horana, is a prestigious school that places a strong emphasis on academic excellence. The school offers a comprehensive and well-rounded curriculum that focuses on three key areas: science, IT, and math. In the science program, students are taught a range of subjects including biology, chemistry, and physics. Through hands-on experiments, students develop a deep understanding of scientific concepts and learn how to apply them to real-world problems. In the IT program, students are taught the fundamentals of computer science and programming languages. They learn how to design and build software applications, websites, and other digital solutions using industry-standard tools and technologies. In the maths program, students are challenged to think critically and logically as they explore the principles of algebra, geometry, and calculus. They learn problem-solving techniques and mathematical modeling that enable them to analyze complex situations and find solutions. Overall, Royal College provides a rigorous academic program that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond. With a focus on science, IT, and maths, students gain the knowledge and skills they need to pursue a wide range of careers in fields such as engineering, technology, medicine, and research.
